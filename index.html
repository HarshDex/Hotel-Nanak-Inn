THE HOTEL

The Nanak Hotel in Bath has fifteen character filled bedrooms set around a historic courtyard.

Formerly a coaching inn, the building was carefully restored in 2024, with each bedroom purposefully unique in its design.

The Hotel has a welcoming space for coffee and a bar that extends out into the courtyard in the warmer months.

Nestled in the heart of Valley, it's an escape in the centre of the Mountains.

THE BEDROOMS


Spread across four floors of the listed property, each bedroom and ensuite combines traditional fabrics from local designers and restored historic features, ensuring no two rooms are the same in style or design.


In each room, luxury elements – comfy king size beds, crisp linen sheets, rainfall showers, fluffy towels, Bramley organic toiletries, Netflix, Roberts Radios, fully stocked mini-bars, complimentary fresh milk and Nespresso coffee machines – guarantee you’ll feel at home.

"Enjoy a fresh morning in the valley, nestled between the beautiful Kali Ganga River and the surrounding mountains."
