THE HOTEL

The Nanak Hotel in Bath has fifteen character filled bedrooms set around a historic courtyard.

PEAKS OF ADI-KAILASH

Adi Kailash, also known as Chota Kailash, is a sacred mountain in Uttarakhand, India, and part of the Panch Kailash group, second in importance after Mount Kailash.

Situated near the Sin La pass, Adi Kailash is revered in Hinduism, with its base camp at Jolingkong Lake. Pilgrims follow two routes—via Gunji or Darma Valley—both leading to the base camp near Kuthi village.

The area also features Om Parvat, another sacred site on the Kailash-Mansarovar route. The first successful ascent was in 2004, with climbers stopping short out of respect for its sanctity.

FACTS & FIGURES

Hotel Nanak is nestled in the picturesque town of Dharchula, amidst the Himalayan peaks of Uttarakhand.

The nearest airport is Pantnagar, about 315 kilometers away, while Tanakpur train station is around 218 kilometers from the hotel. Ideal for exploring nearby attractions, Adi Kailash is 86 kilometers away and Panchachuli Valley is 120 kilometers from the hotel.

Whether you're seeking adventure or a spiritual retreat, Hotel Nanak offers a perfect base for your stay.
